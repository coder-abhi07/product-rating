About

Welcome to Product Rating! I’m Abhishek Kumar, a student at Himachal Pradesh Technical University (HPTU), with a passion for technology and innovation. At Product Rating, we’re dedicated to helping you make informed choices about the products you use. Our platform leverages cutting-edge technology to analyze product images and assess their health and safety based on harmful ingredient detection. How It Works: Simply upload an image of the product you want to evaluate, and our system will process it to identify any harmful ingredients. We then provide a rating on a scale of 100, reflecting the product’s overall safety and quality. This service is available for a wide range of products, from personal care items to food products. Why Choose Us? Comprehensive Analysis: Our tool uses advanced image processing and ingredient matching to give you a clear understanding of a product’s safety. User-Friendly: Uploading an image and receiving a rating is quick and easy. Empowering Choices: Make better decisions about the products you use every day. Join our community, explore, and make safer choices with confidence. Let’s work together to ensure the products you choose are not only effective but also safe for you and your loved ones. Thank you for visiting Product Rating. We look forward to helping you with your product safety needs!
